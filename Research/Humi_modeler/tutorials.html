 How to build a skeleton for a model using HUMI v0.50a:

  • Enter skeleton edition mode by pressing and holding CTRL key.
  • Press the button New Link with the mouse pointer.
  • Press the button Root.
  • Select the Waist01 and Body01 objects in the list. These objects are relative to the same link as they can not move separatly.
  • Press the button Set Joint to indicate you have finished with the object selection and want to start setting the joint position.
  • With the right button of the mouse, click in the middle of the waist, this will be the centre of the model.
  • Switch to the right view by pressing the right arrow key.
  • Move the joint to the centre of the waist with the right button.
  • Release the CTRL key and press and hold it again to start the edition of the next link.

How to animate the model:

  • Hold the SHIFT key.

How to modify the model pose:

  • Enter pose edition mode by pressing and holding SHIFT-F.
  • Pick an object by clicking on it.
  • Click and drag a button (X, Y or Z) to rotate. Left click n' drag to rotate one way and right click n' drag to rotate in the other.
  • When you are ahppy with the stand of the model, press CTRL to save.
